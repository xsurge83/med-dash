Mission

Provide portable, non-invasive cardiac output monitoring for health, wellness, and athletic monitoring. Application areas include: hospitals, clinics, nursing homes, homecare, sports medicine, military, and veterinary markets.

Company Philosophy: Cardiac output is the single most important physiologic assessment which can be obtained from a person. However, the current usage rate for cardiac monitoring is below 1% for those people who could benefit from such monitoring. We will provide our clients with real time beat to beat cardiac output monitoring, allowing our clients to track their health, physical condition, energy expenditure, influence of medication or food intake and much more.

Company Goal: Achieve an ease of cardiac monitoring similar to that which has been achieved for serum glucose monitoring. What once was available only in hospital or research settings will be available to the entire community.
